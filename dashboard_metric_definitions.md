# Dashboard Requirements

## Purpose
Provide leadership and operations teams with a single view of data center health, incidents, capacity, compliance, and improvement actions.

## Dashboard Sections
1. Executive Health Score
2. Incident Management
3. Capacity Utilization
4. Power and Cooling
5. Preventive Maintenance
6. Compliance and Risk
7. Improvement Backlog

## Suggested Visuals
- KPI cards
- Incident trend line
- Severity breakdown
- Rack utilization bar chart
- Power utilization trend
- SLA compliance gauge
- Preventive maintenance completion tracker
- Open risk table

## Filters
- Date range
- Severity
- Zone
- System type
- Incident owner
- Status
